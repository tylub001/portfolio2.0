About Me

I’m Brittany Tylutke, a software engineer and web developer with a passion for building expressive, user-centered digital experiences. After a decade of creating magical moments at Walt Disney World, I transitioned into tech through the TripleTen Software Engineering program, where I mastered HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, version control and GCP deployment.

I specialize in frontend development and creative branding—crafting everything from sleek professional websites to dynamic SaaS applications, designed from inception to deployment and every detail in between. I'm continually sharpening my backend skills and exploring new territory through SQL and Python-based mini games. I thrive on full-stack troubleshooting, seamless API integration, and optimizing layouts for performance, accessibility, and SEO.

I am an enthusiastic and determined go-getter with a passion for code. I thrive on turning ideas into interactive experiences, whether it's crafting pixel-perfect interfaces or debugging backend logic until it sings. With a love for creative problem-solving and a drive to continuously learn, I bring both technical precision and imaginative flair to every project I touch.
